Welcome!

I am currently a master student in the Department of Mathematics at Hacettepe University. My research interests include extremal graph theory, in particular graph coloring, and positional games. I work under the supervision of Lale Ozkahya. In addition, I have been working at TED University as a teaching assistant since 2019.

When I am not working on math, I enjoy playing GO (2 kyu player), reading poetry, backpacking, and camping in nature.

A Special Thank You:

To Nesin Mathematics Village, where I have spent every summer of last six years. The village has provided housing, great lectures, and a wonderful science community.
