Alaittin Kırtışoğlu

Welcome! I am a second-year Ph.D. student in Applied Mathematics at Illinois Institute of Technology, and I am a member of discrete math research group.

I was a full time intern in the summer 2022 for ten weeks administered by ORISE under the sponsorship of NSF at Fermi National Accelerator Laboratory.

Before my Ph.D., I was a master student studying graph theory under the supervision of Lale Ozkahya in the Department of Mathematics at Hacettepe University. At the same time, I was working at TED University as a teaching assistant. My research interests include extremal graph theory, optimization, and positional games. https://exatrkx.github.io/

When I am not working on math, I enjoy playing GO (1 dan player), reading poetry, backpacking, and camping in nature.

A Special Thank You:

To Nesin Mathematics Village, where I have spent every summer of six years. The village has provided housing, great lectures, and a wonderful science community.
